A website created For Parents By Kids(FPBK); A safe place to allow kids the freedom of speech freely in a no judgement zone.

This site is a place of expression for kids. A place to expression their personal experience and viewpoints. A place to guide parents and enhance their family life. Kids are hoping that this safe place of expression will encourage parents to set realistic standards and goals that's important for their growth and their education. By viewing this webiste kids hope parents will continue to instill responsibility, hardwork, good manners, empathy, tolerance independence and creativity. A safe place FPBK.
