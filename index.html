For Parents By Kids

  • (current)

A website created For Parents By Kids, A Safe Place To Allow Kids Freedom Of Speech In A No Judgement Zone.

This site is a place for kids to:

  • Express their viewpoints
  • Share their personal experience
  • Guide parents to open and honest discussion
  • Enhance their family life
Kids are expecting this safe place of expression will encourage parents to set realistic goals that's important for their growth and their education. By viewing this website kids hope that parents will continue to instill responsibility, hardwork, good manners, empathy, tolerance, independence, trust and creativity within them as kids.
